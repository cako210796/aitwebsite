About me

I am an postdoc in the Advanced Interactive Technologies lab at ETH Zurich. My research focuses on human-computer interaction with computer vision technology. Specifically, I am working on gaze estimation with a single webcam. I received my PhD at Max Planck Institute for Informatics, advised by Prof. Andreas Bulling. You can find more information on my personal website.
