About me

I'm a Postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zurich in the Advanced Interactive Technologies lab. Briefly, I am interested in how to incorporate structured information into deep learning pipeline, either semantically or geometrically. Application-wise, I am mainly working on hand-pose/body-pose-shape/human-motion estimation and also learning based view synthesis and 6D pose estimation. If you are interested in Semester/Master projects, please directly contact me by email. Especially, if you are a fan of football, check this link for thesis.
