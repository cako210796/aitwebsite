About me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the Advanced Interactive Technologies lab at ETH Zurich.

I received a B.S. degree and a Ph.D. degree in software engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2018 and 2023, respectively. My advisor is Prof. Feng Xu.

My research interests include dynamic reconstruction, machine learning, and 3D animation.
