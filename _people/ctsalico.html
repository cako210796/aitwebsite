About me

I am a Postdoctoral researcher at the ETH AI Center, affiliated with the Advanced Interactive Technologies lab and the Computational Robotics Lab.

I received my Ph.D. from the Institute of Fluid Dynamics at ETH Zurich, where I developed reconstruction methods for 3D fluid flows. During my Ph.D. I also worked at Google Zurich, where we developed a generative Text-to-3D method for generating realistically-tectured meshes from text input.

In my current research, I am interested in learning material properties from image sequences to accurately simulate physics in AR environments. Applications include digital humans, animation, and measurement techniques. If you are interested in Semester/Master projects, please directly contact me by email.
