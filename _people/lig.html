About me

I am a PhD student in a joint program between Advanced Interactive Technologies Lab , at ETH Zurich and Google under the supervision of Professor Otmar Hilliges and Thabo Beeler. I received my BSc (2018) and MSc (2020) in Computer Science at the ETH Zürich, with a focus on computer graphics. Although my legal name is Gengyan Li, I typically go by Simon.


Research Interests

My primary focus at the moment is in rendering the Eye and Periocular region, currently in the context of neural volumetric method like NeRF. In general, I focus on physically simulation backed neural rendering.
