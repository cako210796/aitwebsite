Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Advanced Interactive Technologies lab at ETH Zurich. My research interests are in the area of computational interaction. More specifically, I use computational optimization methods to support or automate the design of user interfaces. My dissertation particularly considered the optimization of text input methods and studied how people type. Therefore, I worked with Antti Oulasvrita in the User Interfaces group of Aalto University in Helsinki. You can find more information on my personal homepage.
