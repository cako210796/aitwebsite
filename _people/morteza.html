About me

I am a joint Ph.D. student between Advanced Interactive Technologies lab and DisneyResearch|Studios. Prior to that, I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science at ETH Zürich (2022) and my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology (2020).

Research Interests

My research primarily focuses on advancing deep generative modeling for human-based applications, such as generating full-body human images. More specifically, I am interested in enhancing the capabilities of state-of-the-art diffusion models to enable more scalable and controllable content creation.
