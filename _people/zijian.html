Biography

I am a second-year doctoral student at ETH supervised by Professor Otmar Hilliges and Professor Andreas Geiger. Before starting my Ph.D., I obtained my Master's degree in Robotics, Systems and Control from ETH Zurich.

My research interest lies in 3D deep generative models and 3D avatar reconstruction, with a goal to reconstruct and generate realistic virtual humans.
