About Me

I am a Ph.D. student with the Max Planck ETH Center for Learning Systems (CLS), supervised by Michael J. Black (MPI) and Otmar Hilliges (ETH). My research focuses on estimating human body pose and shape from images and videos. Such technology will be a crucial step towards next-gen telecommunication and entertainment, e.g., in immersive 3D video-conferencing and VR gaming.
